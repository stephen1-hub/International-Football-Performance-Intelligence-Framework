# 🌍 International Football Performance Intelligence Framework (IFPIF)

## Overview

As national teams prepare for the 2026 FIFA World Cup, international friendly matches provide valuable insight into team readiness, playing style, attacking efficiency, defensive resilience, and overall performance quality.

This project develops a multi-dimensional Football Intelligence Framework that evaluates 101 national teams across five key performance dimensions and combines them into a Football Intelligence Index.

An interactive Streamlit dashboard was developed to enable users to explore team performance, rankings, and football identities.

## Business Questions

1. Which teams are most prepared for the 2026 FIFA World Cup?
2. Which teams are overperforming or underperforming attacking expectations?
3. Which teams possess the strongest defensive foundations?
4. Which teams produce the most entertaining matches?
5. Which teams play the most intense style of football?
6. Which teams demonstrate the most complete football profile across all analytical dimensions?

## Framework Components

### 🏆 World Cup Readiness Index

Evaluates:

* Points Per Game
* Goal Difference
* Expected Goals For (xGF)
* Clean Sheet Rate
* Match Sample Reliability

### ⚽ Attacking Efficiency Analysis

Measures:

* Goals Scored
* Expected Goals (xG)
* Finishing Efficiency

### 🛡️ Defensive DNA Framework

Measures:

* Goals Against Per Match
* Clean Sheet Rate
* Goal Difference

### 🎭 Entertainment Index

Measures:

* Both Teams To Score Rate
* Over 1.5 Goals Rate
* Over 2.5 Goals Rate
* Average Goals Per Match

### 🔥 High-Intensity Football Index

Measures:

* Corners Per Match
* Bookings Per Match
* Goals Scored
* BTTS Rate

### 🌍 Football Intelligence Index

Combines:

* Readiness
* Attack
* Defense
* Entertainment
* Intensity

into a single team intelligence score.
